Saudi Arabia in the Spotlight as Market Opens to Foreigners

Economy boasts some of the biggest companies in the region

By Michael Ovaska, Asa Fitch
Published June 12, 2015 at 2:00 ET

Saudi Arabia’s economy is dominated by the state-owned energy and government services sectors, but that isn’t reflected in the stock market, where manufacturing and financial services are the biggest sectors...
