You'll never look at food the same way again.

Martin Roller has reimagined everyday objects in a very delicious way. Using salami, carrots and everything in between, Roller proves food isn't just for eating with his fantastical cuisine creations.

This isn't the first time Roller has tried to construct something unique. He transformed slippers into cowboy boots and tires into a birthday cake in a previous photo series.

Confuse your stomach with some of his amazing pieces below and check out his site for the complete series.
