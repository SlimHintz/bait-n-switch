          On a day off from organized team activity practices, Houston Texans defensive end J.J. Watt had pretty fancy plans. He was to present Carrie Underwood at the CMT Music Awards.

          He decided to take a few teammates with him to Nashville.

          The night that Watt, cornerbacks Johnathan Joseph and Kareem Jackson, offensive lineman Ben Jones, punter Shane Lechler and inside linebacker Brian Cushing started Wednesday night with a private plane ride and didn't conclude before Watt met Arnold Schwarzenegger, for whom he did his "I'll be back" impersonation in person.
