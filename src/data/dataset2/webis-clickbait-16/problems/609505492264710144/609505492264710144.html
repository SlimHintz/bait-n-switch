Kris Jenner Totally Owns It In A Plunging Dress

The Huffington Post  |  By Julia Brucculieri
Posted: 06/12/2015 9:40 am EDT Updated: 06/12/2015 9:59 am EDT
PARIS, FRANCE - JUNE 11: Kris Jenner arrives at the 'Costes' restaurant on June 11, 2015 in Paris, France. (Photo by Marc Piasecki/GC Images) | Marc Piasecki via Getty Images

Looks like Kris Jenner's been taking style cues from her daughters.

The matriarch of the Kardashian clan stepped out for a night on the town in Paris on Thursday, and looked amazing.

Clad in a tight black-and-white minidress with a low neckline (Kim would surely approve), the 59-year-old looked seriously stunning -- even her youngest daughter, Kylie, thought so.

The 17-year-old shared a photo of her mom on Instagram with the caption, "Damn ma! I hope I look this good at ur fine age #momgoals."

A photo posted by King Kylie (@kyliejenner) on Jun 11, 2015 at 5:24pm PDT

"Damn, ma" is right. The momager paired her frock with a black overcoat, a pair of strappy black heels and dark oversized sunglasses.

We want a drink from whatever fountain Kris is drinking from, because the woman is seemingly aging in reverse.
