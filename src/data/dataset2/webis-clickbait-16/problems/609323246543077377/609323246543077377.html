    Snoop Dogg wants to be Twitter's new CEO

    By Arjun Kharpal June 12, 2015 3:12 AM
    • 
    • 

      Gangster rapper Snoop Dogg appears to be making a bid for the position of Twitter CEO -- according to his tweets anyway.

      Twitter CEO Dick Costolo stepped down on Thursday after the company has struggled in recent times to expand its user base and grow revenue. Co-founder Jack Dorsey will take up the position on an interim basis.

      But the "Gin and Juice" singer threw his hat into the ring, tweeting using the hashtags #SnoopforCEO.
