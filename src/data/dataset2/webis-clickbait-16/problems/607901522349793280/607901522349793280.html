John Oliver drinks to Sepp Blatter's demise with some Bud Light Lime

1.1k
Shares
Share
Tweet
Share
What's This?
By Andrea Romano2015-06-08 13:14:41 UTC

Last week, as he was reporting on FIFA's corruption, John Oliver made a promise to World Cup sponsors Budweiser, McDonald's and Adidas to publicly endorse their products on his show if FIFA president Sepp Blatter stepped down from the organization.

Just days later, it happened: Blatter resigned. Oliver kept his promise by not only kicking his feet up in bright gold Adidas sneakers, but also chugging a bottle of Bud Light Lime and taking a bite of every single item on McDonald's dollar menu.

"Bah, dah, bah, bah, bah. I'm doin' it, " says Oliver as he took out the tray of greasy fast food. He really is a man of his word.
