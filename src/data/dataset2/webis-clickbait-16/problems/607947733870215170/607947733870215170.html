Miley Cyrus posed nude with a pig for Paper Magazine cover

By Tricia Gilbride2015-06-08 16:25:31 UTC

The cover of Paper Magazine is currently the best place to find weird nude pictures of celebrities, thanks to Kim Kardashian's trailblazing photo shoot.

Miley Cyrus posed for the magazine's summer 2015 cover embracing her pet pig, Bubba Sue, wearing nothing but mud. It's actually sort of shocking that it took so long for this to happen.

See also: 2-year-old and teacup pig are the two best friends that anyone could have

Miley teased the cover, shot by Paola Kudacki, with a charming pig mask snap, which could be one of the "absolutely insane" shots that Paper promises when the full story is published online tomorrow. The physical copy will hit newsstands on June 22.

Bubba Sue is becoming a fixture on the celebrity scene, and has previously partied with with Joan Jett.

The profile will focus on Miley's nonprofit that supports homeless and LGBTQ youth, Happy Hippie Foundation, and sexual fluidity.
