The tattooed topless model set to become a Swedish princess: The VERY racy past of Sofia Hellqvist... who will marry handsome Prince Carl-Philip today

  • Sofia Hellqvist and Sweden's Prince Carl-Philip are having party on the M/S Stockholm ahead of wedding tomorrow
  • Prince Edward and the Countess of Wessex will attend, along with Queen Margrethe and Prince Frederik of Denmark
  • Miss Hellqvist has a racy past that includes stints as a glamour model, which saw her posing topless with a snake
  • Carl-Philip also has a colourful past, including the 'wild summer' of 2012, when he went on a drunken rampage

ByRuth Styles for MailOnline

When she walks down the aisle in the Chapel Royal of the Sveriges Kungahus tomorrow, Sophia Hellqvist, 30, will be taking the final steps towards a new life as Sweden's newest princess.

Watching from the pews will be her parents Erik and Marie, as well as crowned heads from all over Europe - including the UK's Earl and Countess of Wessex and Denmark's Queen Margrethe.

At the altar, Miss Hellqvist will be met by her fiancé, Sweden's Prince Carl-Philip, Duke of Värmland and her partner of almost five years - who today smiled proudly as he arrived with his bride-to-be for tonight's lavish pre-wedding party on the M/S Stockholm yacht.

And while Carl-Philip, 36, is no stranger to scandal, it is Miss Hellqvist's colourful past that proved the initial stumbling block.

Born in Täby, a small colourless town just outside of Stockholm, the future princess moved to Älvdalen, a remote settlement in central Sweden shortly after her sixth birthday.

Her childhood, shared with sisters Lina and Sara, was uneventful and happy, if a far cry from the infinitely more privileged upbringing enjoyed by her fiancé.

Clever and ambitious, Miss Hellqvist was encouraged to excel at school by her Danish father and Swedish mother and, when she decided aged 18 that she wanted to study in New York, went with their blessing.

Once in the Big Apple, she enrolled on an accounting course at the Institute of English and Business, and added modules covering computing and business development to her studies.

But while Miss Hellqvist, who speaks excellent English, clearly benefited from her time in the US, it wasn't until she returned to Sweden to study at the University of Stockholm that life really got interesting.

In need of some extra cash to fund her studies, she landed part time jobs as a waitress and also, fatefully, a glamour model.

Her first shoot came at the age of 20 and saw her posing topless in a pair of camouflage print bikini bottoms and clutching a snake to preserve her modesty.

The photos were published in the sleazily named Slitz magazine where they swiftly proved a hit with the title's readership.

Indeed, so popular did the glamour shots prove, they led directly to Miss Hellqvist being given the dubious honour of being crowned Miss Slitz 2004.

Risqué though the shots were, they had nothing on what came next - a much-publicised stint on reality television show Paradise Hotel the following year.

Originating in the US, Paradise Hotel is best described as a tropical take on Big Brother and sees contestants sent to live in a luxury hotel.

Each week the single contestants are paired off with a new person and have to share their room with them for the next seven days - with predictable results.

In Miss Hellqvist's case, it meant a now infamous scene in which she was seen kissing 41-year-old American porn star Jenna Jameson.

In a separate incident, she was seen relaxing topless in her hotel room while a male contestant massaged her bare backside.

Unsurprisingly, when news of 36-year-old Carl-Philip's new relationship emerged in 2010, the Swedish Royal Family were initially put 'on the defensive' as sources put it at the time.

Queen Silvia in particular is said to have considered Miss Hellqvist an unsuitable match for her son, who at the time, was newly single following a split from his girlfriend of almost a decade, PR girl Emma Pernald.

The two were said to have met in a Stockholm nightclub, shortly after Miss Hellqvist's return from a second stint in New York - this time studying to become a yoga teacher.

Shortly after the official announcement was made, the pair moved into a love nest in the upmarket Djurgården district of Stockholm and still share the same home today.

Since then, Miss Hellqvist has set about toning down her public image and today, is more likely to be spotted wearing a glamorous gown than cavorting in a G-string.

What's more, her husband-to-be is no angel himself.  One of the most controversial members of the Bernadotte dynasty, he earned a playboy reputation early on, thanks to his love of fast cars and glamorous parties.

Two years after meeting Miss Hellqvist came the infamous 'Wild Summer' of 2012 - a slew of colourful incidents still referenced by Sweden's press which delightedly documented his antics.

Among them was a drunken rampage in central Stockholm while wearing an alien mask and a happy slapping incident in Cannes - all captured in grainy mobile phone footage.

He also, following a wild night out, managed to lose his wallet and was forced to hitch-hike to the motor race he was due to compete in, before spectacularly crashing his car on the first lap.

Fast forward to 2015, however, and things look very different. Not only has Miss Hellqvist been welcomed into the Swedish Royal Family, she has also become an increasingly prominent presence at high profile events.

In December, she arrived on a clearly proud Carl-Philip's arm for the Nobel prize giving ceremony in the Norwegian capital Oslo looking every inch the princess in her burgundy gown.

More recently, Miss Hellqvist was spotted taking part in Sweden's National Day celebrations which, along with dressing up in a slightly retrograde outfit, meant taking part in a procession to the Skansen open air museum for speeches and singing.

Now comes the biggest test of all. Seen yesterday arriving at the Sveriges Kungahus for her wedding rehearsal, Miss Hellqvist appeared nervous and was reluctant to talk to waiting Press.

Yet for all her pre-wedding nerves - and provided there are no last minute mishaps - tomorrow will see her walk back down the aisle following the ceremony as Princess Sofia, Duchess of Värmland.

Miss Hellqvist might have a racy past but her very modern fairytale looks set to have a very happy ending.
