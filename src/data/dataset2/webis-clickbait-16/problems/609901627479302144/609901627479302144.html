Photos that could only happen in Jordan
 
 Share

Here at CNN we're heading on a road show through the Middle East, with Amman, Jordan one of our stops.

 

We want you to help us find out the most wonderful and unique things about Jordan.

 

From the food to the views, to the heritage from Petra to the oasis of Wadi Rum, show us your pictures that encapsulate all that is great about the country.

 

Please send us your original images (horizontal shots work best) and you can also take part on Instagram or Twitter by hashtagging your images #CNNJordan -- and your shots could be on CNN!
