(CNN)Will Katniss Everdeen ever stop being awesome?

The first trailer for "The Hunger Games: Mockingjay -- Part 2" debuted Tuesday, offering a sneak peek of what's to come when the film hits theaters this year.

Katniss (played by Jennifer Lawrence) is leading the rebellion against President Snow, the ruthless and dictatorial leader of Panem.

The stars of the film have been whetting fans' appetites for the new movie on social media using the hashtag #Unite.

It's based on the popular "Hunger Games" trilogy of teen novels. The studio split the final book into two parts.
