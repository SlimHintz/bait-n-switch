10 times the cast of 'Orange is the New Black' surprised us with drop-dead glamour

3.4k
Shares
Share
Tweet
Share
What's This?
By Hayley Wilbur2015-06-12 14:48:55 UTC

Orange is possibly the most difficult color to wear. It takes confidence and charisma to rock the bright hues from coral to pumpkin. So it's no wonder that the characters in Netflix's Orange is the New Black have such big personalities to compensate for their lack of sartorial choices.

See also: Laverne Cox will be the first transgender celebrity in Madame Tussauds

Throughout the seasons of the hugely popular show, we've seen a dramatic transformation of cast members who've blossomed from interesting looks to red carpet regulars. In lieu of the third season now out, we compiled our favorite inmate-to-A-list makeovers. See how "Crazy Eyes" Suzanne, Taystee, Pennsatucky, and the others clean up beautifully for a night out.

Orange Is The New Black

  1. 1. Sophia - Laverne Cox

    Only Litchfield County Jail's own hairdresser could look just as fierce behind bars as she is in front of the cameras. Here, Laverne Cox sports Zac Posen at the 2015 GLAAD Media Awards.

  2. 2. Crazy Eyes - Uzo Aduba

    Known for her hair knots and her, well, crazy eyes, Aduba lets her mane flow down, wearing this white hot Christian Siriano sheath to a For Your Consideration OITNB screening.

  3. 3. Piper Chapman - Taylor Schilling

    Taylor Schilling can certainly work both orange and black, flawlessly. Here, she dons this Michael Kors gown with sexy cut-outs to the 2015 Tony Awards.

  4. 4. Taystee - Danielle Brooks

    When she's not working in the library with Poussey, you can find Taystee, aka Danielle Brooks, all glammed as seen here in Christian Siriano.

  5. 5. Alex Vause - Laura Prepon

    We're used to Alex wreaking havoc on Piper, but she also cleans up pretty well. Laura Prepon stuns in Christian Siriano at the 2015 Golden Globe Awards.

  6. 6. Pennsatucky - Taryn Manning

    It is shocking to see Pennsatucky without her signature hoodie and cavity-stricken teeth. Manning dons this Elisabetta Franchi gown to the 2015 SAG Awards.

  7. 7. Dayanara - Dascha Polanco

    What a shame that her prison uniform doesn't flaunt her luscious body. Polanco showcases her amazing figure wearing Michael Costello at the 2014 Macy's Go Red for Women fashion show.

  8. 8. Poussey - Samira Wiley

    Taystee's BFF is sexy chic in this House of CB purple gown at the 2015 GLAAD Media Awards.

  9. 9. Nicky - Natasha Lyonne

    Nicky ditched her signature messy 'do for a side swept look at the 2015 SAG Awards in this jaw-droppingly gorgeous Proenza Schouler ensemble.

  10. 10. Morello - Yael Stone

    Morello never has a lack of red lipstick behind bars. On the 2014 Emmy's red carpet, Stone goes for a more natural look, in a Falguni and Shane Peacock frock.
