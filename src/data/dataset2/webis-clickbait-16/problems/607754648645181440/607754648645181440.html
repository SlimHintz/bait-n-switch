This post contains spoilers from the most recent episode of Game of Thrones season 5.

Is it getting hot in here?

Episode 9 of season 5 of Game of Thrones was fiery to say the least, from Shireen's tragic and traumatic death by fire at the hands of her own father, to Daenerys Targaryen's epic escape with help from her dragon/child Drogon. The penultimate episode did not disappoint on the feels — as per usual — and the Internet was hot (sorry) with reactions.

See also: Who will win the Game of Thrones? The top 8 candidates, ranked
