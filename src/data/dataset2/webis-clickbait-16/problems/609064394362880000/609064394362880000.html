By Sue Ying Tay2015-06-11 13:45:07 UTC

Are you looking for a new career? The Mashable Job Board is the ideal place to search for your next big move. More than 3,000 employers in tech and digital have posted on our board, and they're looking to fill positions from Mashable's community.

Twice a week, we highlight five recently posted openings. Check out some of this week's newest, below, and be sure to read our Job Search Series for valuable career tips.

Position: Jr. Software Developer
Company: Mediaocean
Location: New York, New York

Mediaocean,the acknowledged leader in systems and software for the advertising industry was created to give advertising agencies power over technology in a changing landscape by creating a global, open, and neutral operating system for the advertising business. We are looking for an eager and enthusiastic Jr.Java Developers who wantto use theirskills to develop innovative tools that drive the life-cycle of the advertising buying industry.

Position: Database Administrator
Company: Consensus
Location: San Francisco, California

Consensus is looking for a bright, enthusiastic, hard-working, and creative candidate to join our Information Technology team as a Database Administrator. The ideal candidate will have experience in delivering right-sized and highly available database systems in an Omni-Channel retail environment. The candidate will serve as the DevOps owner for planning and delivering databases in various technologies.

Position: Sales: Account Executive
Company: Mashable
Location: London, UK

Mashable is adding another key position to its growing Sales team. We are now seeking a smart, self-motivated and resourceful individual to join our team as an Account Executive in our London office to help take our thriving sales efforts to a new level. The successful candidate will have an active network of client/agency relationships and a proven track record of building and delivering solid marketing solutions in an effective and scalable way.

Position: Senior Web Frontend Developer
Company: dreamIT GmbH
Location: Hamburg, Germany

Together with a team of 20 passionate geeks and a network of hand-picked service providers we serve since 2011 the entire software development process of the fastest growing online gaming platform in Europe. To reinforce the development team we are looking for a Senior Web Frontend Developer with at least 5 years of professional experience . You are a programmer with a passion, raise high demands on your code quality and build the best web frontends.

Position: Director of Product Development
Company: TheStreet.com
Location: New York, New York

TheStreet, Inc., (www.t.st) an award-winning financial media company recently named on Folio:’s list of Top Places to Work in Media, is seeking a tech-savvy, hands-on and analytically-driven Director of Product Development to join our Marketing Team. This person will be responsible for simultaneously leading the development of new digital products as well as the improvement of existing subscription products and newsletters for the consumer market segment.

Check out more career opportunities on the Mashable Job Board. New positions are posted daily, ranging from entry-level to C-suite positions.
