Rabbit miraculously escapes avalanche

Footage of a snowboarder in Russia captures the moment a rabbit bounds into an avalanche, before amazingly managing to jump its way free

By Helen Coffey, video source Heli-Pro

6:29PM BST 10 Jun 2015

Follow

Comments

Never has the song Run Rabbit Run seemed so apt as in this video of a plucky bunny fighting its way out of an avalanche.

• Snowboarder completely unfazed by falling into crevasse

Filmed in Kamchatka in the far east of Russia, professional snowboarder David Carrier Porcheron was intended to be the footage's focus – before, that is, he was videobombed by a rabbit.


The rabbit seems drawn to the avalanche, like a moth to a flame...

The video was shot by heliski company Helipro, which was making a snowboarding film on Vilyuchinsky Volcano with the Canadian Carrier Porcheron (otherwise known as DCP) and Norwegian pro riders JP Solberg and Terje Hakonsen.

"DCP was riding and caused an avalanche when this crazy rabbit came in our shot," said a Helipro spokesperson.


It shows off its superior leg strength as it bounds to safety

• The 10 best places for heliskiing

The kamikaze creature does appear slightly suicidal as it bounds, seemingly intentionally, into the path of the oncoming snow. Thankfully its powerful hind legs crank into gear and it manages to bound across the avalanche to safety on the other side.
