          FOXBOROUGH, Mass. -- Rob Gronkowski is making a difference in the community once again.

          The energetic, playful New England Patriots tight end took part in the "One Mission" Buzz Off for Kids with Cancer at Gillette Stadium on Sunday morning, shaving his head and then having fun by doing the honors for others. Gronkowski has been a regular at the event in past years.
