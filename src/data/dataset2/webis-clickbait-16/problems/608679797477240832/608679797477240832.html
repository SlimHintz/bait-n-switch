If you don't know who he is, you're not alone.

After a less-than-standout first two seasons for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Matthew Dellavedova finally donned the Superman cape during Game 3 of the NBA Finals on Tuesday night, shooting a playoff career-high of 20 points.
