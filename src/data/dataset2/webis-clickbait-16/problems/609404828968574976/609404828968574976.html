Telegraph Media Group is currently recruiting for an enthusiastic Social Media Content Editor to join the Social Media desk.

They will be responsible for supporting the Telegraph's strategic social media objectives by creating an audience around innovative stories that people love to share and talk about online.

Key responsibilities:

  • Editing and maintaining the Telegraphâ€™s main social media feeds to a high standard.
  • Using real-time analytics to drive decision making on a day-to-day and longer term basis.
