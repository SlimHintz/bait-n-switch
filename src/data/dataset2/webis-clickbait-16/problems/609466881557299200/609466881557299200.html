This Week in Pictures: June 6 - 12, 2015

+
−

An egret bird holds twigs and flies towards a tree to build its nest on the outskirts of Bhubaneswar, India, June 12, 2015. Birds nest their eggs on the beginning of the monsoon season every year.
Biswaranjan Rout/AP Photo

  • An egret bird holds twigs and flies towards a tree to build its nest on the outskirts of Bhubaneswar, India, June 12, 2015. Birds nest their eggs on the beginning of the monsoon season every year.
    Biswaranjan Rout/AP Photo

  • A traditional Mongolian wrestler stands for a picture in front of pictures of famous Mongolian Olympians on June 12, 2015 in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. On July 11-13 traditional Mongolian wrestlers will take part in the Naadam festival which is marked by competitions in the "three games of men" - wrestling, archery, and horseback riding.
    Taylor Weidman/Getty Images

  • A law enforcement officers stands on a road and looks into the forest near Dannemora, N.Y., June 12, 2015. Squads of law enforcement officers are heading out for a seventh day, searching for David Sweat and Richard Matt, two murderers who escaped from Clinton Correctional Facility, a maximum-security prison in northern New York.
    Seth Wenig/AP Photo

  • Workers wearing protective gears spray antiseptic solution as a precaution against the spread of MERS, Middle East Respiratory Syndrome, virus at an art hall in Seoul, South Korea, June 12, 2015.
    Lee Jin-man/AP Photo

  • Firefighter John Fusco gives a drink of water to the dog he rescued from a a second-floor bedroom in a structure fire in Manchester Conn., on June 12, 2015. The dog was taken by an animal control officer to a veterinarian to be checked out.
    Jared Ramsdell/Journal Inquirer/AP Photo

  • Racers jump into the water pit during the women's 3000m steeplechase finals at the Southeast Asian (SEA) Games in Singapore, June 12, 2015.
    Edgar Su/Reuters

  • Russian cosmonaut Anton Shkaplerov rests in a chair outside the Soyuz TMA-15M space capsule after he and U.S. astronaut Terry Virts and Italian astronaut Samantha Cristoforetti landed in a remote area outside the town of Dzhezkazgan, Kazakhstan, June 11, 2015.
    Ivan Sekretarev/Pool/AP Photo

  • Revelers sit in the back of a golf cart as they take part in the Republic of Texas Biker Rally in Austin, Texas, June 11, 2015. The rally is the largest ticketed admission motorcycle rally in the United States and attracts tens of thousands of bikers, organizers say.
    Adrees Latif/Reuters

  • A picture taken on June 11, 2015 shows a tarantula spider sitting on the head of Italian tamer and snake-charmer Bruno Meggiolaro during a show in a circus in Minsk, Belarus.
    Sergei Gapon/AFP/Getty Images

  • Mongolian men and women exercise at a park on June 11, 2015 in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia.
    Taylor Weidman/Getty Images

  • Migrants wait to receive food at the Central railway station in Milan, June 11, 2015. Milan city officials are appealing for help in managing the huge number of migrants arriving from southern Italy after a rescue-at-sea, with many unable to find beds.
    Luca Bruno/AP Photo

  • A woman stands in front of a portrait of a missing child during an exhibition at a square in Nanjing, Jiangsu province, China, June 10, 2015. The exhibition displays 61 portraits of missing children painted by artist Li Yueling. Li hopes the paintings, accompanied by videos and text descriptions, could raise social awareness of China's missing children issue, according to local media.
    China Daily/Reuters

  • Atlanta Braves' Cameron Maybin slides into second base as San Diego Padres shortstop Alexi Amarista jumps to dodge him, June 10, 2015, in Atlanta. The Braves won 4-1.
    John Bazemore/AP Photo

  • Ukraine evacuated hundreds of residents from the site of a series of fuel depot blasts near Kiev that set off a ferocious fire and left several people missing with at least one confirmed dead.
    Sergel Supinsky/AFP/Getty Images

  • A man walks on an empty highway during a national transportation strike in Buenos Aires, Argentina, June 9, 2015. Transportation unions joined left wing parties during a national strike to demand higher wages.
    Victor R. Caivano/AP Photo

  • An injured Syrian man is carried on a stretcher by emergency staff after a barrel bomb attack dropped on a bakery in Ansari neighborhood of Aleppo, Syria, June 9, 2015.
    Anadolu Agency/Getty Images

  • Elementary school students wear masks as a precaution against the MERS virus as they wait for a lesson to start at Midong Elementary School on June 9, 2015 in Seoul, South Korea. South Korea has reported eight deaths related to the virus with 2,500 people quarantined and 1,800 schools closed, June 9, 2015.
    Chung Sung-Jun/Getty Images

  • Battle of Waterloo re-enactors walk near the Lion's Mound during a historical walk for journalists in Waterloo, Belgium, June 9, 2015. On June 19 and 20, the bicentenary of the battle will be commemorated with a reconstruction with 5,000 re-enactors.
    Geert Vanden Wijngaert/AP Photo

  • A mother of a supporter of the Al-Ahly club awaits the verdict against 11 people for their role in a deadly 2012 soccer riot that killed more than 70 people in the city of Port Said, outside a police academy on the outskirts of Cairo, Egypt, June 9, 2015. A Port Said criminal court sitting in Cairo on Tuesday sentenced 11 people to death over a 2012 soccer riot that left more than 70 dead and hundreds injured.
    Hassan Ammar/AP Photo

  • Tampa Bay Lightning goalie Ben Bishop stretches before the second period in game three of the 2015 Stanley Cup Final against the Chicago Blackhawks at United Center in Chicago, June 8, 2015.
    Bruce Bennett/Pool Photo via USA TODAY Sports

  • Vice President for Women and Family Affairs Shahindokht Molaverdi sits under portraits of her predecessors in an interview with The Associated Press at her office in Tehran, Iran, Monday, June 8, 2015. Molaverdi said a limited number of women will be allowed to watch Volleyball World League games in Tehran later this month as it lifts a ban on Iranian women attending male sporting events.
    Ebrahim Noroozi/AP Photo

  • An osprey helicopter lands in the rain near Schloss Elmau hotel near Garmisch-Partenkirchen, southern Germany, June 8, 2015, to transport delegation members traveling with U.S. President Barack Obama from the G-7 Summit, to Munich airport en rout to Washington.
    Carolyn Kaster/AP Photo

  • Members of a U.S. Army burial team fold the American flag that covered the casket containing the remains of World War II U.S Army Air Forces 2nd Lt. Alvin Beethe, of Elk Creek, Neb., during Beethe's full military honors burial service at Arlington National Cemetery June 8, 2015 in Arlington, Va. Beethe's remains were recently identified through DNA testing and returned to his relatives 71 years after his P-38 Lightning failed to return from a mission over Germany.
    Win McNamee/Getty Images

  • Flood waters from the Red River engulf houses in the River Bluff subdivision in Bossier City, Louisiana in a photo released by the Bossier Parish Sheriff, June 8, 2015. A surging Red River that flooded northwest Louisiana this week, affecting hundreds of structures and displacing scores of people, slowly began to recede but was expected to be above flood stage for several more days.
    Lt. Bill Davis/Bossier Parish Sheriff/Handout via Reuters

  • Two Tengananese men fight each other using thorny pandanus leaves on June 8, 2015 in Tenganan Pagringsingan Village, Bali, Indonesia. Every year Tengananese people on the island of Bali celebrate a month long ceremony called 'Usabha Sambah,' to demonstrate respect to Indra, the Hindu god of war.
    Putu Sayoga/Getty Images

  • Indonesia's Victoria Eka Prasetyo serves against Singapore in a sepak takraw match as part of the 28th SEA Games Singapore 2015 on June 8, 2015.
    Action Images/Reuters

  • A moss man plays with his child as he gets ready to take part during the Corpus Christi procession in the small village of Bejar, Spain, June 7, 2015. Men covered from head to toe in moss have paraded through the streets of Bejar to commemorate a daring raid that local legend says helped liberate their town from Muslim occupation some eight centuries ago. Locals believe that during the reign of King Alfonso VIII of Castile (1155-1214) men camouflaged themselves in moss from local forests to enable them to approach the gates of a Muslim fortress.
    Andres Kudacki/AP Photo

  • Babies lie on a mattress in a street during 'El Colacho', the 'baby jumping festival' in the village of Castrillo de Murcia, near Burgos on June 7, 2015. Baby jumping is a traditional Spanish practice dating back to 1620 that takes place annually to celebrate the Catholic feast of Corpus Christi. During the act - known as El Salto del Colacho (the devil's jump) - men dressed as the Devil jump over babies born in the last twelve months of the year who lie on mattresses in the street.
    Cesar Manso/AFP/Getty Images

  • Lightning strikes the Alpine mountains over Garmisch-Partenkirchen as balloons depicting leaders of the G7 countries are inflated in Garmisch-Partenkirchen, June 7, 2015. Leaders from the Group of Seven industrial nations meet on Sunday in the Bavarian Alps for a summit overshadowed by Greece's debt crisis and ongoing violence in Ukraine. Leaders are German Chancellor Angela Merkel, President Barack Obama, French President Francoise Hollande, Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe, British Prime Minister David Cameron, Italian Prime Minister Matteo Renzi and Canada's Prime Minister Stephen Harper.
    Wolfgang Rattay/Reuters

  • John Elmerson of the Philippines falls to the water after slipping on the board, during the men's three-meter springboard diving final at the 28th Southeast Asian Games in Singapore on June 7, 2015.
    Roslan Rahman/AFP/Getty Images

  • Supporters of Turkey's ruling Justice and Development Party wave flags while watching election results in Istanbul,Turkey, late Sunday, June 7, 2015.
    Emrah Gurel/AP Photo

  • A vendor sells balloons in the shape of the Champions League trophy as fans gather in Piazza San Carlo in Turin to watch the UEFA Champions League final soccer match between Juventus and Barcelona on June 6, 2015.
    Marco Bertorello/AFP/Getty Images

  • Gentlemen wear top hat and tails at Epsom racecourse on June 06, 2015 in Epsom, England.
    Alan Crowhurst/Getty Images

  • Zhang Wei, 31-year-old bee farmer from Zizhou County, is covered with a swarm of bees on June 6, 2015 in Yulin, Shannxi Province of China. He achieved a world record of 83.5 kg of bees covering his body in 2011 and the record lasted for four years.
    ChinaFotoPress/Getty Images

  • Eva Kislowski, 9, and Lauren Vernot, 7, hold commemorative programs for former Delaware Attorney General Beau Biden, as the hearse and Biden family arrive for a mass of Christian burial at St. Anthony of Padua Church on June 6, 2015 in Wilmington, Del.
    Mark Makela/Getty Images

  • A participant dressed as an orc from J. R. R. Tolkien's "The Hobbit" walks a dog before the reenactment of the "Battle of Five Armies" in a forest on June 6, 2015 near Doksy village, Czech Republic. Hundreds of Czech enthusiasts re-enacted the battle of men, elves and dwarves against goblins and wargs.
    Matej Divizna/Getty Images
