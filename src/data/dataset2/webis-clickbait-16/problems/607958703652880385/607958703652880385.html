      So, what to expect in an hour? Apple is expected to take on the streaming music providers with its own $10-a-month service that will let users listen to any song or album they choose. This is a clear shot at Spotify, a leader in streaming. Apple  is also expected to revamp its free online-radio efforts with a battery of channels programmed like traditional radio stations, featuring high-profile personalities. The radio station model is more like what Pandora offers. Apple would be bringing more star power to the effort.
