Bill Hader, Danny Pudi and some goats are all Apple fanboys

554

By Neha Prakash2015-06-08 17:36:58 UTC

It's taken a few decades, but Apple finally figured out what makes an entertaining conference.

Apple kicked off Monday's Worldwide Developers Conference with a "behind-the-scenes" Birdman-esque look at how they planned this year's tech conference. Apparently, it involved thoughtful direction by Bill Hader, some pyrotechnics, a couple of drones, and a dance routine from Community's Danny Pudi.

See also: Apple reveals the next generation of OS X: El Capitan

Or, if all else fails: a laptop, a dark stage and a new, shiny gadget will do just fine for Apple fanboys.
